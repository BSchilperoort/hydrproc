The csv files contain the following 6 columns:
    ux   - Horizontal wind speed, x/u component (m/s)
    uy   - Horizontal wind speed, y/v component (m/s)
    uz   - Vertical wind speed (m/s)
    Ts   - Sonic temperature (degree C)
    CO2  - CO2 concentration (mmol m^-3)
    H2O  - H2O vapor concentration (mmol m^-3)

The timestamp of the filenames is in the following format:
    YYYY mm dd HH MM
where
    YYYY is the year
    mm   is the month
    dd   is the day
    HH   is the hour (24hr)
    MM   is the minute
The timestamp refers to the start of the measurements
